The plan must be derived from the estate and live registry authority.

**Per-request authority requirement.** The rerunnable entrypoint must issue a separate HTTP GET to the configured authority URL once per request, including for requests that will later be rejected before stage policy comparison. Caching the authority identity at startup and reusing it across requests is wrong: the test asserts at least one GET per request, and a single shared lookup fails the verifier. Treat each iteration over `requests.json` as needing its own live authority call.
