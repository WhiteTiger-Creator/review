Keep diagnose stateless: every call writes a complete fresh report whether or not a repair has already run. It should only take --dossier and --report; if it's given any other flag, like --input, have it exit non-zero and write no report. `repair --output-dir PATH` rebuilds the rollup from its frozen pre-incident snapshot, re-runs the triage, and writes diagnosis.json and repair_audit.json alongside the three output files, defaulting to /app/output when --output-dir isn't given.

One thing to know about the rollup: all the actual rules — severity normalization and aliasing, how query_id duplicates are collapsed, the dismissal-override windows and their scopes, the near and wide probe families (which round in opposite directions), how anomalies chain together, how reach propagates across chains, and the sequential escalation ledger — live in the review dossier at /app/incident/export_dossier.md.
